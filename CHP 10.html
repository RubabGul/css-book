From Garden To Plate

A potager is french term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden. OFTEN FLOWERS(edible or non-edible) and herbs are planted with
the vegetableto enhance the agrden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aeesthetically pleasing.

What To Plant

Plants are choosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Many are trained to go upward. A well-designed potager
can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potager can disguise their function of providing for a home in
a wide array of forms- from the careless style of cottage to the formality of knot garden.
